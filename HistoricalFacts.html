What are the historical facts about healthcare in Mexico?
If you look at the pie chart above, you can see the amout of people that don't go to the doctor when they are trying to prevent chronic diseases like Cancer, Arthritis, and Cystic Fibrosis. Most public insurance providers don't detect these chronic diseases early on. Private insurance providers offer much better healthcare but at a greater cost most people can't afford.
